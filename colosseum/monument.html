Colosseum - Italy

The Colosseum, also known as the Flavian Amphitheatre, is an ancient Roman amphitheater located in Rome, Italy. It is considered one of the greatest works of Roman architecture and engineering. The Colosseum was used for various public spectacles, such as gladiator contests, animal hunts, mock sea battles, and dramas. Explore the fascinating history of this iconic landmark and marvel at its impressive size and intricate design.
